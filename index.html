About Us


At LJC Vans, we understand that moving can be an incredibly stressful experience. That's why we are here to make your relocation as smooth and hassle-free as possible. With our team of professional movers and a commitment to excellent customer service, we are confident that we can meet all of your moving needs.

Besides our standard removal services, we also offer additional services such as single item relocation or removal, furniture assembly and dissembly, and end of tenancy cleaning. This comprehensive approach allows us to accommodate all of your moving needs under one roof, saving you time, effort, and money.

We understand the importance of transparency and trust. That's why our pricing is competitive and straightforward, with no hidden fees or surprises. We offer free, no-obligation quotes, and our team will work closely with you to create a cost-effective moving plan that fits within your budget.

Thank you for considering LJC Vans as your preferred removals company. We look forward to being a part of your moving journey and making it a success. Contact us today for a free quote or to discuss your moving requirements with our friendly team.
